Privacy Policy

At imagesresizer.tech, your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data and your rights as a user.

1. No Image Storage

We do not save or store your images on our servers. All image processing is performed securely and your files are deleted immediately after processing.

2. No User Tracking

We do not use cookies or tracking technologies to monitor your activity. We do not collect personal information unless you contact us directly.

3. GDPR Compliance

We comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and respect your right to privacy and data protection.

4. Data Deletion Policy

All uploaded images are deleted automatically after processing. No backups or logs of your images are kept.

5. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at support@imagesresizer.tech.
